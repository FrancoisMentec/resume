About me

I fell in love with computer science when I started coding video games in C at the age of 10.

I quickly developed a fascination for computer programming and the problem-solving potential it offers.

Initially, I pursued studies in software engineering, but during the final year of my diploma program, I decided to diversify my skill set by delving into Artificial Intelligence.

This newfound interest led me to pursue a Ph.D. thesis, where I gained valuable experience in scientific research, deep learning, and project management.

Equipped with these skills, I am well-prepared to tackle complex challenges using data science, artificial intelligence, and software engineering.
